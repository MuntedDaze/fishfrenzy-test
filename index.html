Enjoy some of Hobart’s best fish ’n’ chips, seafood and hot chowder on the historic waterfront.

Fish Frenzy is known for quality seafood, grilled dishes, fresh salads and our famous chowder, all served fast with friendly service in a relaxed waterfront setting overlooking the docks and working harbour.

We work closely with our local suppliers and carefully prepare every product to ensure consistently fresh, delicious seafood with outstanding quality and taste.

Dine in or takeaway and experience a true Hobart favourite.
