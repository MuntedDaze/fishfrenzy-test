What We Are

Enjoy the freshest seafood, fish and chips, and hot chowder on Hobart’s historic waterfront.

Fish Frenzy is known for daily fresh fish, grilled seafood, salads, and our famous seafood chowder. Dine in or takeaway available overlooking Hobart’s waterfront.

Fast, friendly service in one of Tasmania’s most iconic waterfront locations.

🏆 TripAdvisor Travellers’ Choice Award (2020)

🏆 Certificate of Excellence (2018)

🏆 Multiple national restaurant awards

Good to Know

We do not take bookings or phone orders. Service is fast and walk-in only.

Gift vouchers are available — please call or email our friendly staff to arrange directly.
